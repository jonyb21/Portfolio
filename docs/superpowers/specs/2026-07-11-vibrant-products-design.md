# Vibrant Homewares and Lighting Refresh

## Design read

Furniture remains quiet, dark, and material-led. Homewares and Lighting provide the expressive side of the portfolio through confident color blocking, tactile mixed materials, and playful but manufacturable forms. The public UI stays dark and restrained so the products remain the visual focus.

## Work cards

- Keep the approved 4:3 mixed-width grid and title overlay.
- Use a compact 16 to 18px title, 16px from the lower edge.
- Limit the readability scrim to the lower 42 percent of the card.
- Keep product images uncropped and clickable.
- Describe the Work page as industrial design across furniture, homewares, and lighting.

## Gallery shot contract

Each product uses one unique lead image, four studio views, and four context views. The supporting views are generated from the approved lead reference.

1. Front or three-quarter form view.
2. Rear or side construction view.
3. Primary interaction detail.
4. Material, joint, control, or light-source detail.
5. Wide context showing scale and purpose.
6. Alternate camera angle or room type.
7. Active state or task context.
8. Human interaction for Homewares, or illuminated human-use context for Lighting.

Every asset is native 4:3 WebP. No two paths, file hashes, camera angles, or close crops may repeat within a product story.
