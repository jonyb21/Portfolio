About

Jon Brooks is a product designer with a strong interest in furniture, broad experience across design development, AI-assisted image generation, cross-platform coding, and custom tools. He has ventured into learning and trying as many AI tools as he can get his hands on, using them to test ideas faster and make each design direction more resolved. He is naturally investigative: when a subject is unfamiliar, he makes it his mission to learn it deeply, test different methods, and turn new ideas into practical design capability. He values that everyone has their own way of working, and stays open to new methods, perspectives, and workflows rather than defaulting to one fixed approach.

Experience
