About

Jon Brooks is an industrial designer and design all-rounder with a genuine love of design. His work spans products, furniture, graphic design, coding, and custom tools, supported by strong hands-on AI experience and a passion for image generation. Self-taught in graphic design, he has experience in catalogue design and supporting marketing work. Curious by nature, he investigates unfamiliar problems, learns from others, and stays open to different ways of working.
